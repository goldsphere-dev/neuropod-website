1. Who We Are

NeuroPod is a neuroscience-based trading discipline platform operated by NeuroPod (Pty) Ltd ("NeuroPod", "we", "us", "our"). We are the responsible party (data controller) in respect of your personal information under the Protection of Personal Information Act 4 of 2013 ("POPIA").

Our platform is accessible at myneuropod.com and app.myneuropod.com.

This Privacy Policy applies to all personal information collected through our website, web application, and related services.

2. What Personal Information We Collect

Information you provide directly

  • Account information: name, email address, password (hashed)
  • Profile information: trader type, experience level, risk profile, trading systems
  • Payment information: billing details processed by Paddle (we do not store card numbers)
  • Waitlist information: email address and optional trading challenge details submitted via the waitlist form

Information generated during use

  • Voice audio: recorded during active trading sessions only, with your explicit consent
  • Voice transcripts: text output from audio transcription
  • Behavioral data: discipline scores, session events, trade records, behavioral patterns
  • Session metadata: session timestamps, duration, pre-session checklist responses
  • Usage data: pages visited, features used, interaction logs

Technical information collected automatically

  • IP address, browser type, device type
  • Cookies and session identifiers (see Section 9)
  • Error logs and diagnostic data

3. Voice Data and Audio Processing

Voice recording is the most sensitive data NeuroPod processes. We have designed strict controls around it.

Key fact: We only record your voice when you have given explicit, informed, separate consent and only during an active session. We never record outside of sessions. Audio is permanently deleted within 60 seconds of successful transcription.

How it works

  • You must actively check a consent checkbox during onboarding before voice features are enabled
  • Recording only activates when your session is in SESSION_ACTIVE state
  • Audio is transmitted to OpenAI's Whisper service for speech-to-text transcription
  • The audio file is permanently deleted from our systems within 60 seconds of successful transcription
  • The resulting text transcript is retained according to your plan's retention period (see Section 6)

What voice data is used for

  • Analysing your behavioral patterns during trading sessions
  • Building and refining your personal voice baseline profile
  • Delivering real-time coaching interventions during sessions
  • Generating post-session behavioral insights

What voice data is never used for

  • Recording outside of active sessions
  • Sharing with third parties beyond the transcription service (OpenAI Whisper)
  • Training AI models (we do not use your data to train models)
  • Sale or commercial exploitation

Withdrawing voice consent

You may withdraw your voice consent at any time from your account settings. Withdrawal stops future recording immediately. It does not affect transcripts already stored — to delete those, use the account deletion process in Section 7.

7. Your Rights

Under POPIA and GDPR principles, you have the following rights regarding your personal information:

Right of access

You may request a copy of the personal information we hold about you. We will respond within 30 days.

Right to correction

If any information we hold is inaccurate or incomplete, you may request that we correct it.

Right to deletion

You may request deletion of your account and associated personal data. To delete your account:

  • Go to Account Settings and select "Delete Account", or
  • Email legal@myneuropod.com with the subject "Account Deletion Request"

Upon deletion: voice recordings, transcripts, and personal data are permanently deleted. Billing records are retained for 7 years as required by South African financial law. Anonymised behavioral aggregate data may be retained.

Right to object

You may object to processing of your personal information for marketing purposes at any time by using the unsubscribe link in any email or by contacting us directly.

Right to withdraw consent

Where processing is based on consent (including voice recording), you may withdraw consent at any time. Withdrawal does not affect the lawfulness of processing prior to withdrawal.

Right to lodge a complaint

If you believe we have handled your personal information in violation of POPIA, you may lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator (South Africa):

  • Website: www.inforegulator.org.za
  • Email: inforeg@justice.gov.za

8. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorised access, loss, destruction, or alteration. These include:

  • Encryption in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest
  • JWT-based authentication with short-lived access tokens
  • Role-based access controls limiting internal data access
  • Automatic deletion of voice audio within 60 seconds of transcription
  • Regular security reviews and audit logging
  • Cloudflare DDoS protection and firewall

In the event of a data breach affecting your personal information, we will notify the Information Regulator within 72 hours and affected users without undue delay, as required by POPIA Section 22.
